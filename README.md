# Ethics Personal Statement
## My Views on Ethics

One of the things I dislike most is when people are not completley honest with me or misleading. That is why when working with and building AI, it is vital to ensure I am completely transparent with my users. 
As people give AI more and more data, we should begin being transparent about potential bias in the model and accuracy of the AI. This becomes even more important as each day passes since we are being forced to use AI more everyday. Moreover, the purpose of the AI and what data is being collected from the user are essential to full transparency.

When making an AI model, I will research the data used to make the model and report on any potential bias. If any are found, the user will be made aware. Thorough testing will also be conducted to see if human oversight makes more accurate decisions. The decision based on these tests will be disclosed to the user as well. Furthermore, I will keep monitoring the AI model with user feedback and report on any flaws done when it reaches a wide userbase. The user will also know what data will be collected from them, and what its purpose will be for. Why the AI is being used, such as to increase accuracy or cut costs, will also be shown to my users.

This will make sure I gain a good relationship built on trust with my users.
